Examples

        

        Example 1  "Scale 1:24,000."

                

                :work1 bf:scale :scale1 .

                        

                :scale1 a gcro:HorizontalScale ;

                           gcro:representativeFractionDenominator “24000”^^xsd:integer ;

                           gcro:scaleAccuracy gcro:ExplicitScale ;  

                           gcro:scaleSource gcro:RepresentativeFraction .

                   

        Example 2 "Scale approximately 1 inch to a mile."

                :work2 bf:scale :scale2 .

                

                :scale2 a gcro:HorizontalScale ;

                        gcro:representativeFractionDenominator "63360"^^xsd:integer ;

                        rdau:P60510 "1 inch to a mile" ;

                        gcro:scaleAccuracy gcro:ApproximateScale ;

                        gcro:scaleSource gcro:VerbalScale .

Example 3 Scale example inaccurately stated: “Scale approximately 1:40,000 not
                1:63,360”

                :work3 bf:scale :scale3a, :scale3b .

                

                :scale3a a gcro:HorizontalScale ;

                        gcro:representativeFractionDenominator "63360"^^xsd:integer ;

                        gcro:scaleAccuracy gcro:InaccurateScale ;

                        gcro:scaleSource gcro:RepresentativeFraction .

                :scale3b a gcro:HorizontalScale ;

                        gcro:representativeFractionDenominator "40000"^^xsd:integer ;

                        rdau:P60510 "Scale approximately 1:40,000 not 1:63,360" ;

                        gcro:scaleAccuracy gcro:ApproximateScale ;

                        gcro:scaleSource gcro:CalculatedFromPrimarySource .

                        

        Example 4 Pictorial View

                :work4 bf:scale :scale4 .

                

                :scale4 a gcro:NotDrawnToScale .

Example 5  Map 1: England Scale bar approximately 1:1,500,000 ;

Map 2: Inset of London - Scale Not Given

                :work5 dcterms:hasPart :part1, :part2 .

                        

                :part1 bf:scale :scale5a .

                        

:scale5a gcro:representativeFractionDenominator "1500000"^^xsd:integer ;

                        gcro:scaleAccuracy gcro:ApproximateScale ;

                        gcro:scaleSource gcro:ScaleBar .

                :part2 bf:scale :scale5b .

                                         

                :scale5b a gcro:ScaleNotGiven ;

                        rdau:P60510 "Scale Not Given." .
